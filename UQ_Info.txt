==========================================
ADMISSION REQUIREMENTS - KEY POINTS
==========================================

MASTER OF ARCHITECTURE:
- BArchDes with GPA 4.5 (UQ) or equivalent
- OR GPA 4.0 + 6 months relevant work experience
- Non-UQ degree: GPA 5.0 in final two design subjects

MASTER OF ENGINEERING PROGRAMS:
- Engineering degree equivalent to Australian bachelor with GPA ≥5.0/7

MASTER OF DATA SCIENCE:
- 2-year option: Degree in CS, IT, maths, stats, engineering, physics with GPA ≥5.0/7
- 1.5-year option: Above + minimum 2 maths, 3 stats/probability, 3 CS courses

MASTER OF CYBER SECURITY:
- GPA ≥5.0/7
- 1.5 years (same background) or 2 years (any background)

MASTER OF INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY:
- Bachelor's degree in field OTHER THAN: Interaction Design, Multimedia Design, HCI, UX Design
- For IT/CS degrees: Only 1 design course completed
- For Design degrees: Only 1 programming course completed
