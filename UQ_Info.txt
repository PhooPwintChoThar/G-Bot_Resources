Architecture and Design
==========================================

Architecture Programs
- Bachelor of Architectural Design (Duration - 3 years)
- Master of Architecture (Duration - 2 years)

• Complete both BArchDes + MArch + professional practical hours (3,300 hours) to become a registered Architect in Australia
• Learn from professional architects and leading researchers who have strong local industry links
• Choose from unique design studio topics and electives – 12 studio topics in M Arch! (e.g. Aboriginal Architecture, Architecture in Australia)

Requirement for MA:
- BArchDes (GPA 4.5) from UQ or equivalent, or GPA 4.0 + 6 months full time relevant postgraduate work experience.
- Non-UQ Bachelor degree requires GPA 5 of the final two design subjects.

Design Programs
- Bachelor of Design
  Majors: Anthropology, Buildings and Environments, Environment and Society, Information Environments, Innovation and Entrepreneurship, Media and Digital Cultures

• Popular as a dual program
• Choose one or two majors, combining multi-disciplinary elements of business, IT, engineering, architecture and the humanities.
• Work on design projects across the entire program.

- Master of Urban Development and Design
  Fields of Study: People and Policy, Infrastructure and Resources, Economics and Strategy
  Mid-Career Salary (Urban Designer): AUD $67K-$101K
  Career Options: Urban Designer, Urban Strategist, Design Advisor, Landscape Architect, Community Engagement Consultant

Computing
==========================================

Bachelor of Information Technology
- Duration: 3 years full time
- Students can choose no major, single major, or two majors

Majors:
1. User Experience Design
   Combining design, creativity, programming and prototyping, UX designers ensure the design of software, websites, or technologies meet their intended use.
2. Software Design
   Learn to create and manage software applications and develop games, apps for mobile devices, and new tools and systems for companies and individuals.
3. Software Information Systems
   Learn to develop and manage databases and information systems.

Bachelor of Computer Science
- Duration: 3 years full time
- Students can choose no major, single major, or two majors

Majors:
1. Cyber Security
   Learn the fundamental processes and practices to protect computing systems from attack, damage or unauthorised access.
2. Data Science
   Our world is recording more data than we can process. Learn to transform data into information and meet the challenges associated with data analysis.
3. Machine Learning
   Master the algorithms that allow computers to do things like automatically identify and harness data for decision making, find hidden insights and predict outcomes.
4. Programming Languages
   Learn how to construct effective programming languages – the building blocks of software.
5. Scientific Computing
   Complex scientific problems require sophisticated algorithms. Study algorithms for mathematical analysis and help to solve the scientific problems of the future.

Computer Science Vertical Dual Degrees (4 years duration)
1. Bachelor of Computer Science/Master of Data Science
   The Bachelor of Computer Science is designed to provide a deeper understanding of all aspects of computer technology. With a Master of Data Science, you will be expertly placed to solve big data challenges across business, social, government and health data.

2. Bachelor of Computer Science/Master of Cyber Security
   The Bachelor of Computer Science will teach you how to create and analyse computer-based systems. With a Master of Cyber Security, you'll be able to look at the field from a different angle, ask bigger questions, and find new ways to tackle real and emerging cyber security threats.
   *The vertical dual degree is only possible with the Cyber Defence or Cryptography fields in the Master of Cyber Security component.

Postgraduate Computing Programs
==========================================

Master of Data Science
- Duration: 2 years
- Most comprehensive data science program in Australia
- Direct industry contact and practical experience
- Industry-based or research capstone project (4-6 units, up to A$9,000 competitive scholarships)
- State-of-the-art facilities, including a big data lab with relevant tools and technologies
- ACS Accredited program

Admission Requirements:
- 2 years requirement: Degree in computer science, IT, mathematics, statistics, engineering, physics, Actuarial Studies area with a GPA of at least 5 on a 7 point scale.
- 1.5 years requirement: In addition to the 2 years option requirement, student must complete minimum 2 mathematics, 3 statistics/probability and 3 computer sciences courses.

Career Information: Data Engineer is No.5 in LinkedIn Jobs on the Rise 2022: The 15 Australian roles that are growing in demand

Master of Cyber Security
- Duration: 1.5 years (same background as field of study) or 2 years (any background) with a GPA of at least 5.0 on a 7 point scale.
- Real-world, practical industry experience embedded via industry or research-based project (6 units)
- State of the art facilities and labs – new Industry 4.0 Energy TestLab
- Comprehensive student activities include UQ Cyber Squad, security operations centre (SoC) experience and Australia Signals Directorate Cyber Experience
- ACS Accredited program

Career Information:
- Cyber Security Specialist is No.9 in LinkedIn Jobs on the Rise 2022: The 15 Australian roles that are growing in demand
- It's estimated that Australia will need an additional 18,000 cyber security professionals by 2026

Master of Computer Science (+ Management)
- Master of Computer Science: 1.5 years
- Master of Computer Science (Management): 2 years
- Required Background: B. Computer Science or B. Software Engineering
- Teaching by a mix of internationally renowned lecturers, industry professionals and leading researchers
- Undertake a research project to address a specific topic or problem
- Choose from more than 50 advanced computer science courses

Industry Demand: Demand for technology workers will grow by 100,000 jobs between 2018 and 2024 (ACS Australia's Digital Pulse 2019, Deloitte)

Master of Information Technology
- Duration: 2 years
- For students without IT/CS background

Admission Requirements:
- Bachelor degree in a field other than: Interaction Design, Multimedia Design, Information Technology, Computer Science, Software Engineering, Computer Engineering
- No more than 5 subjects in CS/IT in bachelor degree
- Articulation possible through Grad Cert (Sem 1 only) or Grad Dip (Sem 2 only)

Master of Interaction Design
- Duration: 2 years
- For students without interaction design background

Admission Requirements:
- Bachelor's degree (or equivalent) in a field other than interaction design, multimedia design, human computer interaction or user experience design.
- For IT/CS/Software Engineering degrees, only 1 design course can be completed
- For Design degrees, only 1 programming course can be completed
